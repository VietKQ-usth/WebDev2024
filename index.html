Missiles fired, bombs dropped in Hanoi live defense drill.

Different segments of the army coordinated to eliminate targets with anti-air guns and missles during a defense drill in Hanoi on Wednesday.

Besides the shooting range, the High Command of the Capital, in cooperation with other units, performed live fire drills at several other locations in Quoc Oai, Ba Vi and Soc Son districts on Hanoi outskirts.
